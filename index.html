Our Mission

At Zonaka Zwivhuya we want all the personnel in the company to be part of the team player, everyone needs to play a part and the role. We want to provide all our clients with the best service or to render them with the best service that they can ever think of. We want to give them the services that will value their money.

Our Objectives

-To ensure customer satisfaction.
-To provide high-quality products and good services to the client at an affordable and marketable rate.
-To contribute to the black economic empowerment.
-To improve and create employment opportunities for the youth of and local community members.

Vision

The vision of Zonaka Zwivhuya General Enterprise is to be the big company that will provide the good and quality of services to the client both in public and private sector, also to be a well established company with qualified and well-trained staff. All the employees will be developed with good, needed skills to service the client.
